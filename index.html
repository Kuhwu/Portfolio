Projects

Vinaria

Vinaria is an action-adventure game developed in Unity. The game features an isometric perspective and follows the journey of Ashianna in a mysterious world filled with exploration, combat, and puzzle-solving.

Jemnobili

Jemnobili is an android game developed in Unity, you take on the role of a Green Dinosaur, defending its home from the relentless pack of Blue Dinosaurs. The action unfolds as you navigate the Green Dinosaur's precaurious position in a rope.

Spire Climb

Spire Climb is a 2D Android game developed in Unity, where you play as a character that scales the walls of a spire. The goal of the game is to climb the structure while trying to avoid and dodge various elements, such as falling debris, obstacles, and other entities that may cause the character to stop or fall.

Your day and Night

Your Day and Night is a digital sanctuary developed in Kotlin for self-reflection and personal growth. This project goes beyond traditional journaling, providing a space where you can chronicle your thoughts, milestones, and aspirations in a way that resonates with you.

Project Cath

Project Cath is a image recognition system that is currently work in progress and it is being developed in Python, it uses machine learning to detect and identify objects within images. This project will involve creating a model for object detection and building a user-friendly interface to interact with the system.
